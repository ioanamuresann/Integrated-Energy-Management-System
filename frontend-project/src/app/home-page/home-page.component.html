Welcome to the Energy Management System

This Energy Management System is designed to efficiently manage users and their associated smart energy metering devices. The system provides access to two types of users after a secure login process:

  • Administrator (Manager): Administrators have the privilege to perform CRUD (Create-Read-Update-Delete) operations on user accounts, smart energy metering devices, and the mapping of users to devices. Each user can own one or more smart devices in different locations.
  • Clients: Clients can utilize the system to monitor and manage their smart energy metering devices.

The system enables seamless management of user accounts with attributes including ID, name, and role (admin/client). Smart energy metering devices are defined by their ID, description, address, and maximum hourly energy consumption.

Enjoy a streamlined experience as you navigate through this user-friendly Energy Management System.
